Our Story

From Code
to Craft

After two decades as a software engineer, I discovered something unexpected — the most rewarding work isn't writing elegant code. It's shaping raw wood into something beautiful and useful with your own hands.

Every piece begins with carefully selected timber. We work with local Thai hardwoods and imported species, choosing each board for its grain, character, and strength. No shortcuts, no particle board — just honest wood and traditional joinery.

"Wood has a way of teaching patience. Each grain tells you where it wants to go — you just have to listen."
The Maker
Founder & Craftsman
